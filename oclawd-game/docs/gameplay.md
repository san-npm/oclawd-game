## Overview

Oclawd is a strategic space conquest game where you build fleets, establish stations, mine resources, and compete for galactic dominance. All actions are recorded on-chain on Base Sepolia.

---

## Core Mechanics

### üöÄ Ships

Your fleet is the foundation of your empire. Each ship is a unique NFT with distinct attributes.

#### Ship Types

| Type | Role | Strength | Best For |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| **Fighter** | Combat | High attack, low defense | Raids & PvP |
| **Transport** | Hauling | High cargo capacity | Trading resources |
| **Cruiser** | Balanced | Medium attack & defense | Exploration |
| **Battleship** | Heavy combat | Highest attack & defense | Fleet warfare |

#### Ship Attributes

- **Attack Power** - Damage dealt in combat
- **Defense Power** - Damage resistance
- **Speed** - Movement across the galaxy
- **Fuel Capacity** - Range of travel
- **Resource Capacity** - How much cargo can be carried

#### Rarity Tiers

Ships come in four rarity levels, each with stat multipliers:

| Rarity | Drop Rate | Stat Bonus |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Common | 60% | Base stats |
| Rare | 25% | +25% stats |
| Epic | 12% | +50% stats |
| Legendary | 3% | +100% stats |

---

### üè≠ Stations

Stations are your bases in space. Build them to mine resources, produce ships, and expand your territory.

#### Station Types

1. **Mining Station** - Extracts resources from asteroids
2. **Production Station** - Builds new ships
3. **Trading Post** - Buy/sell resources with other players
4. **Defense Platform** - Protects your territory

#### Station Limits

- Maximum 10 stations per player
- Each station costs 0.01 ETH to build
- Stations generate passive income

---

### ‚õèÔ∏è Resources

Resources are the lifeblood of your empire.

#### Resource Types

- **Minerals** - Used for ship construction
- **Energy** - Powers stations and ships
- **Technology** - Enables upgrades and research
- **Credits** - Universal currency for trading

#### Mining

1. Position a ship near an asteroid field
2. Initiate mining operation
3. Resources are deposited to your wallet as ERC20 tokens
4. Trade on the marketplace or use for building

## Progression System

### Experience Points (XP)

Earn XP through:
- Minting ships (+10 XP)
- Building stations (+50 XP)
- Mining resources (+5 XP per operation)
- Trading (+2 XP per transaction)
- Combat victories (+25 XP)

### Levels

| Level | XP Required | Unlock |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | 0 | Basic ships |
| 5 | 500 | Transport ships |
| 10 | 2,000 | Cruisers |
| 20 | 10,000 | Battleships |
| 50 | 100,000 | Legendary ship crafting |

## PvP Combat

### Combat Resolution

1. Attacker initiates battle
2. Ships compare attack vs defense
3. Speed determines first strike
4. Damage is calculated with randomness factor
5. Winner takes resources, loser loses ship (or ship takes damage)

### Combat Formula

```
Damage = (AttackPower √ó RandomFactor) - DefensePower
RandomFactor = 0.8 to 1.2
```

## Leaderboards

Compete globally across multiple categories:

- **Fleet Power** - Total combined ship stats
- **Resource Wealth** - Total resources owned
- **Territory** - Number of stations
- **Combat Score** - PvP victories
- **Overall Rank** - Combined score

## Tips for New Players

1. **Start with Fighters** - Cheap and effective for early game
2. **Build a Mining Station first** - Passive income is key
3. **Don't over-extend** - Protect what you own before expanding
4. **Check the Marketplace** - Sometimes buying is cheaper than mining
5. **Join alliances** - (Coming soon) Team up for protection
