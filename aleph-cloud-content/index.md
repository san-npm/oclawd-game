## Content Strategy

### Target Audience

**Primary**:
- CTOs and technical leaders
- DevOps and cloud engineers
- AI/ML professionals
- Startup founders and product managers

**Secondary**:
- IT managers and operations teams
- Business executives evaluating cloud options
- Developers and engineers
- Researchers and academics

### Key Messages

1. **Cost Savings**: 50-70% savings compared to traditional cloud
2. **No Lock-in**: Freedom to move between providers
3. **Performance**: Global edge deployment for reduced latency
4. **Flexibility**: Pay-per-use, no long-term contracts
5. **Innovation**: Access to cutting-edge distributed infrastructure

### SEO Strategy

Each article targets a specific keyword:
- "decentralized cloud computing"
- "web3 cloud infrastructure"
- "cost-effective cloud storage"
- "gpu rental decentralized"
- "blockchain cloud services"

Keywords are naturally integrated throughout content with:
- Meta descriptions optimized for search
- Headers and subheaders containing target phrases
- Strategic keyword placement in first 100 words
- Natural language integration without keyword stuffing

### Call-to-Action Strategy

All content includes clear, action-oriented CTAs:

- **Twitter**: Direct links to aleph.cloud
- **LinkedIn**: "Ready to explore?" prompts with links
- **Articles**: Dedicated conclusion sections with CTAs

All CTAs drive traffic to [aleph.cloud](https://aleph.cloud).

## Usage Guidelines

### Social Media

**Twitter/X**:
- Post 1-2 times per day
- Mix content types (benefits, use cases, announcements)
- Engage with comments and replies
- Use platform-native visuals when possible

**LinkedIn**:
- Post 1-2 times per week
- Share during optimal times (Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM)
- Engage with comments and discussions
- Share from personal profiles for better reach

### Blog Articles

- Publish 1-2 articles per month
- Promote across all social channels
- Repurpose content into social media posts
- Use in email newsletters
- Share with partners and communities
